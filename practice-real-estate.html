REAL ESTATE AND CONSTRUCTION

  • Preparing Agreements and other documentation in respect of construction and development and re-development of residential and commercial buildings.
  • Conducting due diligence and title verification in respect of acquisition and disposal of agricultural and non-agricultural lands, structures, buildings, flats, commercial and industrial units and issuing title reports and certificates relating to property titles.
  • Advising clients in letting or leasing all types of properties, including conducting negotiations, preparing agreements and other documentations.
  • Representing clients in various types of litigation matters involving real estate.
  • Structuring and advising on real estate development projects.
  • Providing inputs on matters related to zoning, land use under various reservations and regulation of land use and allied laws relating to construction and redevelopment of properties and housing societies.
  • Assisting developers, owners and housing societies on RERA Registration of their projects, their obligations and liabilities under RERA, the amendments required to their legal documentation and the changes needed to their project
  • Advising real estate consultancies on their rights and obligations as real estate agents under the RERA.
  • Advising allottees under the RERA about their rights under the RERA Act.
  • Preparing documentations in terms of Development Agreements, Joint Venture Agreements, Memorandum of Understanding (MOU), Agreements for Leave and License, Tenancy Agreements, Lease Deeds, Deed of Assignment, etc.
  • Assisting with proceedings for the summary eviction of non-cooperative tenants and occupants.
